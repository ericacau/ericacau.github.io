About me

I'm a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence in the National Ph.D. Program in Artificial Intelligence for Society at the University of Pisa. My research interests include detecting and analyzing online discussions to identify the unfolding of polluted environments, such as echo chambers. Her work includes the simulation of the effects of information pollution through Large Language Models.

I'm also a member of the KDD Lab, a joint initiative of ISTI-CNR and the University of Pisa.

Research interests

  • Large Language Models

    Large Language Models applied to opinion dynamics.

  • Social Network Analysis

    Data-driven analyses of social network interaction through Network Science and Natural Language Processing.
